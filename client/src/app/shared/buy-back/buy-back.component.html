We Make It Simple For You !!!

NEO Cellular wants to buy your broken screens! We pay competitive prices for a variety of broken screens. Sell your broken or unwanted screens to us for in-store credit or PayPal. All broken screens sent to NEO Cellular must have a batch number and batch documentation for your shipment. Batch number must be labeled clearly on the box or shipping label, or the package may be returned to sender. All screens will be tested by NEO Cellular upon arrival to verify working LCD and Digitizer. Those found defective will be recycled unless otherwise stated by seller.


Step 1
Fill Out The Form

  • 1. Tell us the number of LCD screens you’re sending
  • 2. Tell us you’d like to process your shipment

Step 2
Shipping Address

  • Give us your shipping and return address

Step 3
Pack Them Up

  • Print out the address label provided on the confirmation page. Affix this label on your package. We even pay shipping if you’re sending 25 or more!

Step 4
Get Funded

  • After the package delivery, allow 3-5 business days for testing before fund disbursement
